Designing a space for our trainee community

MBCT brings together neuroscientists, computer scientists, engineers, and clinicians who are passionate about understanding the brain and building technology that amplifies human potential. This site turns that spirit into a living resource where every trainee can showcase their work, find collaborators, and learn from one another.

What to include in your bio

Start with a concise summary of your current role and research focus. Follow with the key questions or technologies you explore, list collaborators or labs, and close with ways the MBCT community can connect with you.

Capture the voices shaping MBCT

Interviews add narrative texture to our community. Pair trainees with faculty mentors, alumni, or visiting collaborators to document insights and advice for future cohorts.

Spotlight conversations

Structure interviews around open-ended questions that reveal research journeys, turning points, and the tools people rely on.

Share media formats

Host interviews as videos, podcasts, or written Q&As. Provide transcripts to keep the content accessible.

Connect to action

Include links to projects, labs, or papers mentioned in each conversation so readers can follow up.
